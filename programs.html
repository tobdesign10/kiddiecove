Our Programs

Discover age-appropriate learning experiences designed to nurture your child's growth, creativity, and love for learning at every stage of their early development.

Age-Based Learning Programs

Our carefully crafted programs are designed to meet children where they are developmentally, providing the perfect balance of learning, play, and social interaction.

Toddlers Program

Ages 18 months - 2.5 years

Our toddler program focuses on exploration and discovery through safe, structured play. We help your little one develop language skills, social awareness, and motor coordination in a loving, nurturing environment.

What We Offer:

  • Sensory exploration activities
  • Basic social skill development
  • Language and communication building
  • Creative art and music experiences
  • Indoor and outdoor play
  • Potty training support

Schedule Options:

Full-time and part-time options available
Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Pre-School Program

Ages 2.5 - 4 years

Our pre-school program builds confidence and independence through hands-on learning experiences. Children develop critical thinking skills, creativity, and social-emotional intelligence through structured play and guided activities.

What We Offer:

  • Early literacy and numeracy skills
  • Science exploration and discovery
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Dramatic play and storytelling
  • Physical development activities
  • Social skills and cooperation

Schedule Options:

Full-time and part-time options available
Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Pre-Kindergarten Program

Ages 4 - 5 years

Our pre-kindergarten program prepares children for the transition to elementary school. We focus on academic readiness, independence, and confidence-building through engaging, curriculum-based activities.

What We Offer:

  • Kindergarten readiness skills
  • Beginning reading and writing
  • Math concepts and problem solving
  • Science experiments and projects
  • Leadership and responsibility
  • School transition preparation

Schedule Options:

Full-time and part-time options available
Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

What Makes Our Programs Special

Qualified Educators

All our teachers are certified in early childhood education and committed to ongoing professional development.

Low Ratios

Small class sizes ensure personalized attention and meaningful relationships between teachers and children.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our play-based curriculum covers all developmental domains: cognitive, physical, social, and emotional.

Family Partnership

We maintain open communication with families and provide daily updates on your child's progress and activities.
