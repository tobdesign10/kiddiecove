Meet Our Team

Our passionate educators and staff are dedicated to providing the highest quality care and education for your children. Each team member brings expertise, warmth, and genuine love for helping children grow and learn.

Leadership Team

Tobi Asekunowo

Director & Founder

With over 15 years in early childhood education, Tobi founded KiddieCove with a vision to create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive. She holds a Master's in Early Childhood Development.

Maria Rodriguez

Assistant Director

Maria brings 12 years of experience in curriculum development and teacher training. She ensures our programs meet the highest educational standards while maintaining a fun, engaging environment.

Our Teachers

Emily Chen

Toddler Teacher

Emily specializes in toddler development and has a gift for helping little ones feel secure and loved. She creates engaging sensory activities that promote learning through play.

David Thompson

Lisa Park

Pre-Kindergarten Teacher

Lisa prepares children for their transition to elementary school with patience and expertise. She focuses on building confidence and independence in each child.

Michael Davis

Physical Education Specialist

Support Team
